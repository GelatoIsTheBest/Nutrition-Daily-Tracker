# A simple calendar-look Nutrition tracker

This is meant to feel more like a handbook website that serves as a companion to your favorite AI.

I'm using Claude to track my daily nutrition. I simply tell Claude the portions of food I ate and upload a screenshot of the nutrition label.

I've found that this helps me keep track of what I eat every day and makes it much easier to decide what to eat.

This app also includes a DRI calculator, which you can use as a baseline for your daily nutrient intake. You can adjust the goals to fit your own needs.
